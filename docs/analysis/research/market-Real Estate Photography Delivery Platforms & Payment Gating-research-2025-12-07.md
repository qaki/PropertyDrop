## 1. Problem Validation: The "Chasing Payments" Pain Point

**Conclusion:** The problem is Validated and Acute.

Research confirms that payment friction is a major pain point for real estate photographers. While some practitioners are paid upfront, many others are caught in the "chase" cycle, which is a stressful drain on time and resources.

### Evidence of Pain
Photographers frequently discuss:
*   The need to send polite reminders and the awkward escalation to stronger demands.
*   Resorting to calling the client's broker to force payment.
*   The fact that escalating to a broker is seen as an effective, though aggressive, solution highlights the level of frustration.

### Evidence of Market Solution Demand
The most common solutions mentioned by established photographers align exactly with PropertyDrop's core value proposition:
*   "Never deliver anything before payment."
*   "Pay when booking is the way."
*   "My backend system shows watermarked photos to clients, but they don't get the photos until payment is complete."

**Analyst Insight:** Revenue Assurance through Pay-to-Download is not just desiredâ€”it is the industry best practice. Successful photographers have already adopted it, often through custom or clunky solutions. This strongly validates the MVP focus.

## 2. Competitive Landscape

**Conclusion:** Direct competitors exist and feature parity is high.

The core features of Pay-to-Download and MLS Resizing are standard offerings across the main competitors.

## 3. Pricing Validation

**Conclusion:** A $45 - $69/mo price point is validated and competitive, but the **pricing model** is the critical differentiator.

### Market Pricing Models
*   **Subscription (Tiered by Volume):** Aryeo ($100+/mo for high volume), Rela ($49-$99/mo + fees).
*   **Pay-Per-Project/Credit:** HDPhotoHub ($5/project), Tourbuzz ($3/delivery).

### PropertyDrop Advantage
*   **Price Validation:** The proposed $45 - $69/mo flat rate is highly competitive against the starting tiers of major competitors.
*   **Model Advantage:** The "Unlimited" flat rate for MVP is a massive **Simplicity Differentiator**. It avoids the confusion of credit systems and the "penalty" of volume tiers found in competitors.
